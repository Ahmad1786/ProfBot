User Guide

Below you will find information and answers to questions you may have.

This chatbot is designed to answer any questions you might have regarding classes at Rowan University and any upcoming events. Our coolest feature is our schedule maker. It allows you to enter the course numbers you are interested in and it will make a schedule for you! You can also ask the chatbot to give you a description of a course, prerequisities for a course, and particular sections of a course. Lastly, it can answer some questions pertaining to infrastrucutre, departments, and even upcoming events!
